About us

We created Milliondog because we love dogs. Milliondog symbolizes the importance of a dog for his owner and our philosophy reflects just that.

Each Milliondog Cosy is unique in material and colour and emphasises the individual personality and uniqueness of your pawesome darling.

We love our work and you can see this in every Cosy.
